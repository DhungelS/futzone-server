# Futzone
Futzone is an interactive full-stack web application that allows users to have access to wide variety of information on the latest soccer
matches. Users have the ability to select from a collection of notable leagues around the world. Need to find the scorline on a champions league 
match that you didn't catch yesterday? It's there. Or maybe, you are just tired of scrambling through youtube to find the highlight
videos on your favorite matches? Well we have that too. All at your disposal with a click of a button. You can leave reviews, and ratings 
on matches depending on the quality of the match.
